Sermon 62: Surely, there is a strong protective shield of Allah...
==================================================================

*When Amir al-mu'minin was warned of being killed by deceit, he said:*

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ومن كلام له (عليه السلام)
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
لمّا خُوِّف من الغيلة
  </p>
</blockquote>

Surely, there is a strong protective shield of Allah over me. When my
day would come it would get away from me and hand me over to death. At
that time neither an arrow would go amiss nor a wound would heal up.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَإِنَّ عَلَيَّ مِنَ اللهِ جُنَّةً حَصِينةً، فَإِذَا جَاءَ يَوْمِي
انْفَرَجَتْ عَنِّي وَأَسْلَمَتْنِي؛ فَحِينَئِذٍ لاَ يَطِيشُ السَّهْمُ
وَلاَ يَبْرَأُ الْكَلْمُ.
  </p>
</blockquote>
