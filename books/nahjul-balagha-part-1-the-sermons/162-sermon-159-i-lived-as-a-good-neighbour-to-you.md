Sermon 159: I lived as a good neighbour to you…
===============================================

*Good behaviour with his subjects and ignoring their faults*

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ومن خطبة له (عليه السلام)
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
يبيّن فيها حسن معاملته لرعيّته
  </p>
</blockquote>

I lived as a good neighbour to you and tried my best to look after you,
and I freed you from the snare of humbleness and the fetters of
oppression through my gratefulness for the little good (from your side)
and closed my eyes to your many misdeeds which my eyes had observed and
my body had witnessed.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وَلَقَدْ أَحْسَنْتُ جِوَارَكُمْ، وَأَحَطْتُ بِجُهْدِي مِنْ
وَرَائِكُمْ، وَأَعْتَقْتُكُمْ مِنْ رَبَقِ الذُّلِّ، وَحَلَقِ
الضَّيْمِ، شُكْراً مِنِّي لِلْبِرِّ الْقَلِيلِ، وَإِطْرَاقاً عَمَّا
أَدْرَكَهُ الْبَصَرُ، وَشَهِدَهُ الْبَدَنُ، مِنَ الْمُنْكَرِ
الْكَثِيرِ.
  </p>
</blockquote>
