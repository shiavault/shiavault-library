Sermon 40: A true statement to which a false meaning ...
========================================================

*When Amir al-mu’minin heard the cry of Kharijites that “Verdict is only
that of Allah” he said:*

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
ومن كلام له (عليه السلام)
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
في الخوارج لما سمع(عليه السلام) قولهم: "لا حكم إلاّ لله"
  </p>
</blockquote>

A true statement to which a false meaning is attributed. It is true that
verdict lies but with Allah, but these people say that (the function of)
governance is only for Allah. The fact is that there is no escape for
men from ruler good or bad. The faithful persons perform (good) acts in
his rule while the unfaithful enjoys (worldly) benefits in it. During
the rule, Allah would carry everything to end. Through the ruler tax is
collected, enemy is fought, roadways are protected and the right of the
weak is taken from the strong till the virtuous enjoys peace and allowed
protection from (the oppression of) the wicked.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
قال (عليه السلام):
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
كَلِمَةُ حَقٍّ يُرَادُ بِهَا بَاطِلٌ! نَعَمْ إِنَّهُ لا حُكْمَ إِلاَّ
للهِ، ولكِنَّ هؤُلاَءِ يَقُولُونَ: لاَ إِمْرَةَ، فَإِنَّهُ لاَبُدَّ
لِلنَّاسِ مِنْ أَمِير بَرّ أَوْ فَاجِر، يَعْمَلُ فِي إِمْرَتِهِ
الْمُؤْمِنُ، وَيَسْتَمْتِعُ فِيهَا الْكَافِرُ، وَيُبَلِّغُ اللهُ
فِيهَا الاْجَلَ، وَيُجْمَعُ بِهِ الْفَيءُ، وَيُقَاتَلُ بِهِ
الْعَدُوُّ، وَتَأْمَنُ بِهِ السُّبُلُ، وَيُؤْخَذُ بِهِ لِلضَّعِيفِ
مِنَ الْقَوِيِّ، حَتَّى يَسْتَرِيحَ بَرٌّ، وَيُسْتَرَاحَ مِنْ فَاجِر.
  </p>
</blockquote>

Another version
---------------

When Amir al-mu’minin heard the cry of the Kharijites on the said
verdict he said:

I am expecting the verdict (destiny) of Allah on you.

Then he continued:

As for good government the pious man performs good acts in it, while in
a bad government the wicked person enjoys till his time is over and
death overtakes him.

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وفي رواية أُخرى أنّه(عليه السلام) لمّا سمع تحكيمهم قال: حُكْمَ اللهِ
أَنْتَظِرُ فِيكُمْ.
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
وقال:
  </p>
</blockquote>

<blockquote dir="rtl">
  <p>
أَمَّا الاْمْرَةُ الْبَرَّةُ فَيَعْمَلُ فيها التَّقِيُّ، وَأَمَّا
الاْمْرَةُ الْفَاجرَةُ فَيَتَمَتَّعُ فِيهَا الشَّقِيُّ، إلى أَنْ
تَنْقَطِعَ مُدَّتُهُ، وَتُدْرِكَهُ مَنِيَّتُهُ.
  </p>
</blockquote>
