7- THE RIGHT OF YOUR HAND
-------------------------

And the right of your hand is that you stretch it not toward that which
is unlawful to you. Should you do so, you will be chastised by God in
the future. And you are not secure from the blameful tongue of the
people now, either. Do not prevent your hands from performing what God
has made obligatory for them. You should honor your hands in such a way
as to prevent them from engaging in many of the deeds that are not
allowed for them. You should let them engage in many deeds that are not
harmful for them. If they are now used by the intellect and with honor
now, then they are bound to receive a good reward in the future.
